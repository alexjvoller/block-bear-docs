## Pivot and Progress

We've revised how you find, seek, and onboard to coaches and their training programs.

<Columns cols={2}>
  <div>
    Before
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Find A Coach">
        Trawl through various social media platforms to find a coach in your
        field.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Assess">
        Send DMs to find their services. See if what they're offering is right
        for you in terms of cost, time-commitment, progression level
      </Step>
      <Step title="Negotiate">
        Discuss if time, goals and services can be modified to fit your schedule
        and needs
      </Step>
      <Step title="Onboard">
        Download a new app, use a a new sheet, input your data from scractch
      </Step>
      <Step title="Train">
        Use a new journal, most likely a Google Sheet or Template app, with
        heavy manual data-input requirements
      </Step>
      <Step title="Discard">
        When you move coaches, or switch services, your data doesn't come with
        you.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </div>
  <div>
    After
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Find a Training Block">
        Search the marketplace for training blocks. Filter by sport, cost, time,
        progression-level and included services.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Schedule or Train">
        Either schedule the training block in the future or get going straight
        away. The block is imported to your journal so no need to reset your
        peferences.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Progress">
        Build on your previous progession. The starting point for future
        training blocks will reference your latest data points.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
