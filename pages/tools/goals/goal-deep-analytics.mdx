The following analytics are availble in your display options:

<ResponseField name="minimum">
  If a tolerance is set, the Min Trendline will be your lower guard rail.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="maximum">
  If a tolerance is set, the Max Trendline will be your higher guard rail.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="target">
  Your calcualted trendline between now and the end of the block
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="actual">
  Overlay your measured data on top your goal trendlines.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="actual linear">
  You calculated actual trendline so you can compare your actual vs target
  progression.
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="forecast">
  Based on your actual trendline we'll calculte your forecast target at the end
  of the block. This is the data you need to make adjustments if required.
</ResponseField>
