### change details about you

<Steps>
  <Step title="">
    the bits about you table section contains various data points about you
    potentially required through your blocks. This is common particularly for
    calculations which require inputs other then measured or activity data
    points such as "gender" or "dob". All of these fields will initially appear
    blank until required; however you can add your data directly by clicking on
    the row and filling out the input field modal.
  </Step>
</Steps>
